1. $$ \frac{3x^2 - 12x}{6x} = \frac{3x(x - 4)}{6x} = \frac{x - 4}{2} $$

---

2. $$ \frac{x^2 - 4}{x^2 - 2x} = \frac{(x - 2)(x + 2)}{x(x - 2)} = \frac{x + 2}{x} \quad x \neq 2 $$

---

3. $$ \frac{4x^2 + 12x + 9}{2x^2 + 3x} = \frac{(2x + 3)^2}{x(2x + 3)} = \frac{2x + 3}{x} \quad x \neq -\frac{3}{2} $$

---

4. $$ \frac{5x^3 - 10x^2}{15x^2 - 30x} = \frac{5x^2(x - 2)}{15x(x - 2)} = \frac{x}{3} \quad x \neq 0, 2 $$

---

5. $$ \frac{x^2 + 2x - 8}{x^2 - 4} = \frac{(x + 4)(x - 2)}{(x + 2)(x - 2)} = \frac{x + 4}{x + 2} \quad x \neq \pm 2 $$

---

6. $$ \frac{6x^2 + 5x - 4}{3x^2 - 2x - 1} = \frac{(3x - 2)(2x + 2)}{(3x - 2)(x + 1)} = \frac{2x + 2}{x + 1} \quad x \neq \frac{2}{3} $$

---

7. $$ \frac{x^2 - 9}{x^2 - 6x + 9} = \frac{(x - 3)(x + 3)}{(x - 3)^2} = \frac{x + 3}{x - 3} \quad x \neq 3 $$

---

8. $$ \frac{2x^3 + 10x^2 + 12x}{4x^2 + 8x} = \frac{2x(x^2 + 5x + 6)}{4x(x + 2)} = \frac{x + 3}{2} \quad x \neq 0, -2 $$

---

9. $$ \frac{x^2 - 5x + 6}{x^2 - 9} = \frac{(x - 2)(x - 3)}{(x - 3)(x + 3)} = \frac{x - 2}{x + 3} \quad x \neq \pm 3 $$

---

10. $$ \frac{4x^3 - 16x^2 + 16x}{2x^2 - 8x} = \frac{4x(x^2 - 4x + 4)}{2x(x - 4)} = \frac{2(x - 2)^2}{x - 4} \quad x \neq 0, 4 $$
